About

Founder Dave Dresden has 22 Billboard hits as a member of EDM act Gabriel & Dresden. He has 25 years of experience in the trenches of the music industry. His experience spans all aspects of the business, including media, A&R, radio, production and performance.

Dresden Research is a research firm in the music space that consists of active performing artists as well as data scientists. Our perspective is informed by fans directly. We are performing or creating original music in the studio, so we know the trends before most others even know what's going on.
