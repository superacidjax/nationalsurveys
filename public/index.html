Powerful Insights

We provide you with detailed analysis on the artists most aligned with your brand objectives. We can help you find the most effecive brand ambassador to target your brand's ideal demographic. Our periodic surveys on musical tastes identify trends affecting your brand.

We spot trends before they happen. We can determine artist popularity as they relate to particular demographic segments. We know when an artist or musical genre is gaining or losing popularity.

Find New Customers

Dresden Research can define a music genre as well as individual artist in demographic and lifestyle terms. Then, we source these target demographics across mailing lists, email lists, and social media to identify matching potential customers.

Targeted Marketing

We know how to reach the right potential customers with the right message at the right time. Dresden Research can assist your brand in choosing between alternative approaches by leveraging our surveys and data mining. Our knowledge and contacts within the music industry give us a unique advantage.

Predictive Marketing

Our access to industry data and analytics allows us to accurately forecast marketing initiatives. We can predict success and failure for different scenarios given your plans, goals and available resources. We work with established industry data sources as well as our own proprietary data systems.
